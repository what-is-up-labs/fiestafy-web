Fiestafy

Party's on. BYOMusic.

Now available for iPhone.


Collaborative Playlists Via Tweets

Let your party guests add to the queue by tweeting an artist and song name to your party playlist that you've set up on the Fiestafy app!

Host A Party

Start your party playlist by creating your unique party #hashtag in Fiestafy.

Invite Guests

Guests can add songs instantly by tweeting a song & artist to @fiestafymusic with your party's #hashtag!

Party On

See what's in the queue, and control the music from the app. Keep adding jams and dance off!

About Fiestafy

Fiestafy was created for MobileWeek 2014 Hackathon by Matt Grasser, Emily ! Duong, Christopher Guess, Eduardo Ramirez, Sam Krueckeberg & Nick Ihm. It uses the Spotify and Twitter API.
